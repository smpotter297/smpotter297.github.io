How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Take out two pieces of bread and lay them flat
  2. Open the peanut butter and jelly jars
  3. Take a knife and spread peanut butter on the left piece of bread evenly
  4. Then clean the knife and spread jelly on the right piece of bread evenly
  5. Then press the two pieces of bread together with the peanut butter side and the jelly sides facing each other
  6. Finally, cut the sandwich in half diagonally
